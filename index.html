Introduction

Hello, my name is Ojasvi Sharma. I am a creative individual with a strong interest in design and visual storytelling. I enjoy expressing ideas through art and exploring different mediums such as oil pastels, acrylic painting, and sketching.

Along with my creative pursuits, I am also actively involved in sports and music. I am a lawn tennis and table tennis player, and a trained classical vocalist. These experiences have helped me develop discipline, focus, and a sense of rhythm, which I bring into my creative work.

I am currently pursuing my studies through Zakir Husain Delhi College (DU) while preparing for NIFT, where I have successfully cleared Round 1. My goal is to build a career in Communication Design, where I can combine creativity with purpose to create meaningful and impactful visual experiences.

I am constantly experimenting with new techniques and ideas to improve my skills and develop my own unique style.
