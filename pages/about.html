Who Am I?

I’m Ojasvi Sharma, an artist working across both traditional and digital mediums. From charcoal sketches and pencil work to bold oil, acrylic, and pastel paintings, I focus on creating visuals that stand out.

I also explore digital art and graphic design, blending creativity with modern tools to build work that feels both expressive and contemporary.
